About Me

I am a 2nd year MTech CSE (RA) student at IIT Hyderabad, researching optimization strategies for the AMD Xilinx Versal ACAP architecture. My work focuses on hardware acceleration for deep learning models like CNNs, GNNs, and ViTs, improving efficiency and minimizing power consumption. I previously worked as an SDE-1 at CSG International and completed my undergraduate studies at SJB Institute of Technology, Bengaluru.
